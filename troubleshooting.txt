Problem: AcmePhone 15 won't charge.
Solution:
  1. Check if the charging cable and adapter are working properly.
  2. Try a different outlet or power source.
  3. Clean the charging port on your phone.
  4. Restart your phone.
  5. If the issue persists, contact Acme Support.

Problem: AcmeWatch Pro not syncing with my phone.
Solution:
  1. Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on both devices.
  2. Check if the devices are within range of each other.
  3. Restart both devices.
  4. Unpair and re-pair the devices.
  5. If the issue persists, contact Acme Support.
